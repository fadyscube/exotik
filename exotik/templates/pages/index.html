What does Exotik do?

1. Random activities

Exotik generate for you random activities. And gives you new ideas if you want to make something different than everyday.

With Exotik, you can manage your tasks with the todo list, and you can get different todo list recommandations depending on your obligatory tasks.

3. Calendar

You can visualize your tasks in a calendar to have a global view on your time, and to plan your future tasks and give each day a main goal.

The Exotik game is a funny and basic game to play when you are bored.
